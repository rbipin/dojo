About

I am a passionate software developer with extensive experience in Azure and AWS cloud platforms, specializing in building scalable, cloud-based solutions. Problem-solving is at the core of what I do—I thrive on designing and architecting applications that are efficient, resilient, and future-proof.

Currently, I work as a Principal Software Consultant at HTC Global Services , where I collaborate with Meijer, Inc. to design and develop scalable solutions in the Azure cloud. My role involves architecting robust systems for Meijer's Inventory Management System, ensuring efficiency, reliability, and scalability. I also provide technical and business guidance to the team, helping drive strategic decisions and best practices.

Previously, I have worked at Rocket Mortgage and United Wholesale Mortgage, where I held various positions and contributed to projects at different stages of the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC). From initial architecture and design to development, deployment, and optimization, I have played a key role in delivering high-quality software solutions.

Outside of work, I enjoy spending time with family and friends, whether it's playing board games, video games, or exploring new technology. I have a passion for traveling and photography. Whenever I get the chance, I love being outdoors, especially kayaking and embracing nature.
